Our Mission

Bridging the gap between surplus and opportunity

At the intersection of industrial efficiency and educational equity lies a missed opportunity. Every year, corporations grapple with outdated technology, industrial scrap, and warehouse overflow. At ESurplus Inc., we redirect it into the hands of students eager to build tomorrow.

The Circular Model

From corporate warehouse to university classroom

We have pioneered a unique ecosystem that turns your surplus into a high-impact R&D pipeline, administered with precision and care.

01

Corporate Donation

Your company transfers surplus, decommissioned tech, or industrial scrap to ESurplus Inc. — no extra logistical burden on your end.

02

Sort & Prepare

Our specialized team meticulously classifies, cleans, and prepares each material — determining its educational potential or resale value.

03

Education in Action

Materials reach UAZ students who deconstruct, analyze, and innovate — learning hands-on at the pace of the global tech market.

04

Impact & Return

Salvageable components re-enter the economy via our auction platforms. The cycle closes. The planet wins. The future wins.

What Students Learn

Reverse Engineering for the Future

Under ESurplus guidance, UAZ students engage in hands-on R&D that would otherwise be cost-prohibitive. They emerge with the experience necessary to enter the global tech workforce.

Deconstructing

Ask Us Anything

We accept a wide range of industrial and corporate surplus — including decommissioned hardware, electronic scrap, warehouse overflow, outdated technology, and industrial byproducts. If you're unsure, just reach out and we'll assess it together.

Beyond the clear logistical benefit of having your surplus managed professionally, you become a named patron of education and social equity. We provide impact reports, CSR recognition, and a meaningful story to share with your stakeholders and community.

Absolutely none. Every student in the program receives access to materials, mentorship, and learning resources completely free of charge. That is at the core of our mission — removing the financial barrier between talented students and real-world experience.

Items that aren't suited for direct educational use go through our specialized eBay stores and auction platforms, where they are resold responsibly. Nothing goes to a landfill. The circular model ensures maximum value is extracted from every single item.
